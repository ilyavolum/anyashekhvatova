ANIA SHEKHVATOVA
CV

Born 1990 in Maiysky (North Caucasus, Russia)

Lived in Moscow, Russia until 2022

Currently lives and works in Lisbon, Portugal


Education:

__2020-2021 Online institute Sreda Obucheniya, specialty artist

__2009-2012 Moscow State University, faculty sociology

__2013-2014 Russian State University for the Humanities, faculty sociology


Anya Shekhvatova is a multidisciplinary artist who works with thought-provoking installations that explore the complexities of the human experience. Her pieces are a peculiar mixture of science, tradition, and sensory stimulus.

She touches upon the themes of a human's place in the world, their sense of self, the connection between the spiritual, sensual, and physical. Through the use of temporality and ritualistic elements, she creates an ever-evolving narrative that challenges the viewer's perception of the world. As she creates her art, Anya investigates how a person's viewpoint can be altered by the unique environment they are immersed in.

Anya employs a range of media, including traditional materials and new technologies, to create a multi-sensory experience for the viewer. Anya's approach to sensory perception extends beyond sight and sound, encompassing taste, touch, and smell to create a truly immersive artistic encounter.
