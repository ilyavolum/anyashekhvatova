Random coffee experiment 4, NII, Moscow, 2019

The experiment took place in a nightclub during the daytime, when the club was not operating. These circumstances encouraged the guests to perceive the new in the familiar. This experiment included a large number of distracting details, which made it closer to having a real life coffee tasting, where the conditions are not always perfect.
